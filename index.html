Our Donate Plan

Donate for these plans, and help poor and disadvantaged people to ensure a better life for them.

Food for a family

Food is the most essential requirement for sustenance of human life. With your donation, we will try to ensure food for every poor family.

Shelter for a family

Shelter is one of the basic needs of human existence. With your donation, we will try to ensure shelter for every poor family.

Education for a child

Education is an essential requirement for every child. With your donations, we will try to ensure children's education by providing free books, snacks, school uniforms and bags, toys, etc.
